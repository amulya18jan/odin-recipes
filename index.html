Red Sauce Pasta Recipe (Restaurant-Style)

  • ⏱️ Prep Time: 10 mins
  • ⏱️ Cook Time: 20 mins
  • 🍽️ Serves: 2–3

Ingredients:

For Pasta

  • 200 g pasta (penne, fusilli, or spaghetti)
  • 4–5 cups water
  • 1 tsp salt

For the red sauce

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4–5 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 4 large tomatoes, pureed (or 1 cup tomato puree)
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup (optional, for slight sweetness)
  • 1 tsp red chili flakes
  • 1 tsp mixed Italian herbs/oregano
  • Salt, to taste
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
  • ¼ cup water

Optional vegetables

  • Bell peppers (capsicum), sliced
  • Mushrooms
  • Sweet corn
  • Olives

For garnish

  • Grated cheese (Parmesan or mozzarella)
  • Fresh basil or parsley

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta
    • Bring water to a boil and add salt.
    • Add pasta and cook until al dente (firm to the bite).
    • Drain and reserve ¼ cup pasta water.
  2. Prepare the sauce
    • Heat olive oil in a pan.
    • Sauté garlic for 30 seconds.
    • Add onions and cook until translucent.
    • Add tomato puree and cook for 8–10 minutes until the raw smell disappears.
  3. Season
    • Add chili flakes, oregano, black pepper, salt, and sugar.
    • Stir in ketchup (if using) and a little water.
    • Simmer for 3–4 minutes.
  4. Add vegetables (optional)
    • Toss in bell peppers, mushrooms, or other vegetables.
    • Cook for 2–3 minutes; keep them slightly crunchy.
  5. Combine
    • Add cooked pasta to the sauce.
    • Mix well and add a splash of reserved pasta water if needed.
    • Cook together for 1–2 minutes.
  6. Serve
    • Garnish with cheese, basil, and extra chili flakes.
    • Serve hot with garlic bread or a salad.

Tips

  • For a richer flavor, roast the tomatoes before blending.
  • Add a tablespoon of cream for a creamy tomato pasta variation.
  • Use fresh basil for an authentic Italian taste.

Enjoy your homemade red sauce pasta! 🍝😋
